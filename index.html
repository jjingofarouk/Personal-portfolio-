About

Let's create something amazing together. I'm a dedicated web developer with five years of experience turning ideas into reality. My skill set spans both front-end and back-end development. Back in 2021, I decided to try my hand at creating an application that would connect patients with communicable diseases but living in remote areas to available health workers. Fast-forward to today, and I've had the privilege of successfully building this and more socially impactful software solutions that will benefit millions of people.

I'm currently dedicated to pioneering a revolutionary healthcare solution. My focus is on developing a secure, decentralized network that enables seamless exchange of health data between institutions. In parallel, I'm building a cutting-edge predictive health analytics engine. By leveraging vast datasets on genetics, lifestyle, and environmental factors, this engine aims to accurately predict disease risks and empower proactive preventive measures.

When I'm not engrossed in the digital world, I find joy in giving back to my community. I volunteer my time to provide basic healthcare and health education to those in need. Beyond that, I'm a passionate chess player, enjoying the strategic challenge with my wife. And when I'm not immersed in these activities, I'm often found exploring the latest medical advancements at the regional referral hospital. To top it off, I'm dedicated to several other social impact projects, which occupy a significant portion of my time.
